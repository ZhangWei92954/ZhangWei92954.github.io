About Me

I am Wei Zhang, a Researcher at Shenzhen University General Hospital. My research focuses on drug delivery, reversal of chemoresistance, and tumor microenvironment regulation in pancreatic cancer and other malignant tumors. I aim to integrate materials science, pharmaceutics, artificial intelligence, and traditional-medicine-derived natural products to develop more effective and translational antitumor therapeutic strategies.

Research directions:

  1. Construction of novel biomimetic drug delivery systems: developing cell-membrane-biomimetic particles, hybrid metal frameworks, biomedical materials, and other new drug delivery systems.
  2. AI-driven nanomedicine design: using machine learning and deep learning algorithms for rational and intelligent design of nanomedicines with efficient delivery capability.
  3. Development of natural-product delivery systems and antitumor mechanism studies: delivering traditional Chinese medicine-derived natural products with intelligent delivery systems and investigating their antitumor mechanisms.
